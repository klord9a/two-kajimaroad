CONCRETE

PAVEMENT

Concrete Pavement is closely related with our life and environment. We can identify and offer you the most suitable pavement to protect your life and preserve the good environment. In the quality control of concrete pavement, we use special machines and tools to control the flatness and height that matches the standard height. In addition, we place importance on the strength and durability of the standard, and evaluate the quality based on the appropriate test method and inspection decided in advance.
